🔭 Long-Term Vision

The proposed platform is envisioned not merely as a monitoring system but as a national digital infrastructure for distributed renewable energy management. By combining IoT technologies, cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and transparent governance mechanisms, the platform can support Bangladesh's transition towards a smart, efficient, and sustainable energy ecosystem — while contributing to the national targets of renewable energy expansion and carbon emission reduction.
